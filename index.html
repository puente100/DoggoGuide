FAQ

Does DoggoGuide work offline?
DoggoGuide requires an internet connection to fetch breed lists and images from the Dog CEO API.

Where do images come from?
Images are provided by the public Dog CEO API.

Do you track me or collect personal data?
No. DoggoGuide does not collect personal information. See the Privacy section below.

Which iOS version is supported?
iOS 14 or later. We recommend the latest iOS for the best experience.

How can I request a feature?
Send us an email or open a GitHub issue with details of the feature you’d like.

Is there a cost?
DoggoGuide is free. No subscriptions, no ads.

Troubleshooting

  • Images not loading? Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, the Dog CEO API might be experiencing downtime.
  • App shows an error message? Close and reopen the app. If the problem continues, contact support and include your iOS version and device model.
  • Slow performance? Ensure you are on Wi‑Fi or a stable cellular network.

Privacy Policy

DoggoGuide respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or sell personal information. The app fetches public data (breed lists and images) from the external Dog CEO API.

  • Data we collect: None. DoggoGuide does not collect personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Third‑party services: Dog CEO API provides public dog images and breed data. Requests to that service may include your IP address as part of normal internet communication.
  • Crash/Analytics: Not collected in the current version.
  • Children’s Privacy: DoggoGuide is general‑audience content and does not target children under 13.

If our data practices change, we will update this page and the in‑app disclosures.

Data Deletion

DoggoGuide does not create user accounts nor store personal data on our servers. If you emailed us and wish to delete your message or any related data, contact us and we will delete it from our inbox.
